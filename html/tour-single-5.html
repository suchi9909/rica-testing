highlight

World-class shopping, dining and entertainment attractions, beautiful beaches, mysterious deserts and culturally-rich heritage sites Dubai is a must-see holiday destination, with something for everyone. From shopping enthusiasts and adventure-seekers to foodies and families

this bustling city has an eclectic mix of activities and places to suit all tastes. Whether you're looking to embark on a gourmet holiday or a sightseeing trip, explore our action-packed travel itineraries to get the best out of your Dubai visit.

inclusion

  • Return economy class airfare with taxes
  • dubai to abu Dhabi Internal flight ticket
  • 5 Nights Stay With Breakfast At dubai
  • Normal Visa Charges of dubai

exclusion

  • Any Extra Sightseeing Which Is Not Mentioned In The Itinerary
  • Overseas Travel Insurance & Personal Expense Such As Mineral Water etc
  • Personal expenses
  • Excess baggage charge

itinerary

Arrive at Abu Dhabi airport, complete immigration & collect your luggage. Later transfer to Dubai (2.5 Hours). Arrive and check in to the hotel. Check in time is 3 PM.Later in the evening proceed visit Marina Dubai with Dhow cruise.
  • dinner
After breakfast proceed to visit Dubai City Tour with entrance to Dubai Museum & Monorail Ride to Atlantis The Palm Hotel. Arrive back to hotel and time free for rest.Late afternoon proceed for Desert Safari inclusive of Dunes Bashing, Camel Ride, Menhdi, Tea/Coffee, Snacks, Cold Drinks, Sheesha, Belly Dance, Tanoura Show & Barbeque Dinner.
  • breakfast & dinner
Today after breakfast time is free till afternoon to relax. You may enjoy hotel ameneties.Later in the evening visit Dubai Mall & Worlds Tallest Burj Khalifa Tower. Enjoy amazing views from 124th Floor of Burj Khalifa Tower. See the magnificent Musical Fountain Show at Dubai Mall.
  • breakfast & dinner
Morning after breakfast check-out from hotel and proceed to Abu Dhabi. Arrive at Abu Dhabi and check-in at Yas hotel. Later proceed to visit Yas Waterworld or Warner Bros.
  • breakfast, lunch & dinner
Today we proceed to Cordoba. On arrival we visit Mezquita from inside followed by orientation city tour which includes Roman bridge, Arab waterwheels and Plaza del Potro. Then we leave for Valencia.
  • breakfast, lunch & dinner
Enjoy sumptous breakfast at Yas Viceroy Hotel. Later proceed for Abu Dhabi City Tour and visit to The Grand Mosque. Later proceed to Abu Dhabi airport to board a flight.
